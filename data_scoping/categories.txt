1) Academic Calendar
    - Includes:
        o Start/End of semesters
        o Exam periods
        o holidays
        o Registration deadlines
    - Typical questions: 
        o "When do exams start?"
        o "When is the next semester?"
        o "When are holidays?"
    - Data sources: 
        o Usually PDF documents
        o Published yearly by the institution
    - Nature:
        o Semi-static(changes once per academic year)
2) Social Services(SAS)
    - Includes:
        o Canteen menus
        o Accomodation
        o Scholarships
        o Social support services
    - Typical questions: 
        o "Whats todays menu?"
        o "How do I apply for schoolarship?"
        o "Where are student residences?"
    - Data sources: 
        o Mostly HTML pages
        o Some PDFs for regulations
    - Nature:
        o Dynamic + Static mix
            ~ Menus --> dynamic
            ~ Rules --> static
3) Tuition fees(Propinas)
    - Includes:
        o Fee amounts
        o Payment deadlines
        o Payment methods 
        o Penalties for late payment
    - Typical questions: 
        o "How much is tuition?"
        o "When do I need to pay?"
        o "What happens if I miss a payment?"
    - Data sources: 
        o HTML pages or PDFs
    - Nature:
        o Semi-static
            ~ Usually updated once per academic year
4) Course Descriptions(FUCs)
    - Includes:
        o Course content
        o Learning objectives
        o Evaluation methods
        o Creadits (ECTS)
        o Professors
    - Typical questions: 
        o "What is this course about?"
        o "How is it evaluated?"
        o "How many ECTS does it have?"
    - Data sources: 
        o Mostly PDF documents
    - Nature:
        o Static
            ~ Rarely changes during the year
5) General Regulations
    - Includes:
        o Academic rules
        o Student rights and duties
        o Exam rules
        o Attendance policies
    - Typical questions: 
        o "How many times can I fail?"
        o "What are the exam rules?"
        o "Can I appeal a grade?"
    - Data sources: 
        o Official PDF regulations
    - Nature:
        o Static
            ~ Changes very rarely

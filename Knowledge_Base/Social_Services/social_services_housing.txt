The Social Action Services (SAS) have 5 Residences located on the Campus in Bragança, with capacity to accommodate 355 students. They are intended primarily for scholarship students, as defined in the social action system of higher education institutions.

The residences offer a range of amenities, ensuring comfort and well-being in the use of the facilities, as well as the conditions needed to concentrate on studying, contributing favourably to academic success.

SASIPB Residences:

- Residência Verde, made up of Blocks A/B/C/D, on the Santa Apolónia Campus – 5300-252 Bragança – 200 beds;

- Residência da Escadaria, a single Block on the Santa Apolónia Campus – 5300-252 Bragança – 108 beds;

- Residência Gulbenkian, a single Block on Av. Sá Carneiro, no. 320 – 5300-252 Bragança – 47 beds;

All Residences have:

- single, double and triple rooms;

- central heating;

- internet access;

- kitchenettes for preparing light meals;

- study and common rooms;

- a weekly laundry service for bed linen and towels;

The accommodation assignment process is carried out annually. The residences are governed by a Residence Regulation, which sets out, among other things, the conditions for admission and use of equipment, internal discipline rules, and other aspects.

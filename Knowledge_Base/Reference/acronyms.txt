IPB abbreviations and programme codes. Students often write the short code instead of the full name; treat the code and the full name as the same thing when searching for timetables, exams, syllabus or course information.

Schools
- ESTiG = Escola Superior de Tecnologia e Gestão (School of Technology and Management)
- ESA = Escola Superior Agrária (School of Agriculture)
- ESE / ESEB = Escola Superior de Educação (School of Education)
- ESSa = Escola Superior de Saúde (School of Health)
- EsACT = Escola Superior de Comunicação, Administração e Turismo (School of Communication, Administration and Tourism, in Mirandela)

Common terms
- CTeSP = Curso Técnico Superior Profissional (short-cycle professional course)
- Licenciatura = bachelor's degree (1st cycle)
- Mestrado / MSc = master's degree (2nd cycle)
- UC = Unidade Curricular (course unit / subject)
- FUC = Ficha de Unidade Curricular (the course unit sheet that lists the syllabus and the assessment/grading rules)
- ECTS = European credit units
- propinas = tuition fees; horário = timetable; exames / época = exam season; sumários = lesson summaries
- SAS = Serviços de Ação Social; GRI = Gabinete de Relações Internacionais; cantina = canteen

Degree codes students commonly use (code = full Portuguese name = English)
- EI / LEI = Engenharia Informática = Computer Engineering
- CD = Ciência de Dados = Data Science
- EEC = Engenharia Eletrotécnica e de Computadores = Electrical and Computer Engineering
- EGI = Engenharia e Gestão Industrial = Industrial Engineering and Management
- EM = Engenharia Mecânica = Mechanical Engineering
- EC = Engenharia Civil = Civil Engineering
- EQ = Engenharia Química = Chemical Engineering
- EER / ER = Engenharia de Energias Renováveis = Renewable Energy Engineering
- TB = Tecnologia Biomédica = Biomedical Technology
- TDG = Tecnologias Digitais e Gestão = Digital Technologies and Management
- GNI = Gestão de Negócios Internacionais (curso europeu) = International Business Management
- GES = Gestão = Management
- CONT = Contabilidade = Accounting

These bachelor and master programmes in engineering, technology and management are taught at ESTiG. Agriculture and biosciences are at ESA; education, sport and social work at ESE; health and nursing at ESSa; tourism, administration and communication at EsACT.

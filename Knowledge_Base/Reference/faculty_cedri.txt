IPB faculty and research areas — CeDRI (Research Centre in Digitalization and Intelligent Robotics).

CeDRI is an IPB research centre working on computer science, electrical and computer engineering, industrial engineering, robotics, artificial intelligence and applied mathematics. General contact: cedri@ipb.pt, phone (+351) 273 303 200. Full member list: https://cedri.ipb.pt/people/integrated-members

IPB key contacts and offices. When the specific answer is not available, direct the student to the most relevant office below. Give the email and phone number when they are listed here.

Central services
- Academic Services (Serviços Académicos) — enrolment, registration, tuition payment, certificates, diplomas, academic calendar. Website: https://ipb.pt/pt/estudar/estudantes/servicos-academicos
- Social Action Services (SAS) — scholarships, student residences, canteen, sport, medical and psychological support. Phone: +351 273 303 361 / +351 273 330 899. Email: sas@ipb.pt. Website: https://sas.ipb.pt
- International Relations Office (IRO/GRI) — Erasmus, international students, mobility, double diplomas. Phone: +351 273 330 690. Email: sylwia@ipb.pt. Website: https://ipb.pt/pt/internacionalizar/erasmus
- Image and Student Support Office (GIAPE) — new-student support, integration, academic activities. Phone: +351 273 303 390. Email: giape@ipb.pt
- Health Office (Gabinete de Saúde) — nursing, dietetics, psychology, social and dependency support. Phone: +351 273 303 278 (Bragança), +351 278 263 914 (Mirandela).
- IPB switchboard: +351 273 303 000. Website: https://ipb.pt

The five schools of IPB (contact the school that runs your programme for timetables, exams, course content and a professor's contact)
- ESTiG — Escola Superior de Tecnologia e Gestão (engineering, technology, management), Campus de Santa Apolónia, Bragança. Website: https://estig.ipb.pt
- ESA — Escola Superior Agrária (agriculture, biosciences, veterinary, environment), Campus de Santa Apolónia, Bragança. Email: esa@ipb.pt. Website: https://esa.ipb.pt
- ESE — Escola Superior de Educação (education, teaching, sport, social work, communication), Campus de Santa Apolónia, Bragança. Email: eseb@ipb.pt. Phone: +351 273 303 000. Website: https://www.ese.ipb.pt
- ESSa — Escola Superior de Saúde (nursing, gerontology, pharmacy, dietetics), Avenida D. Afonso V, 5300-121 Bragança. Email: essa@ipb.pt. Phone: +351 273 330 951. Website: https://essa.ipb.pt
- EsACT — Escola Superior de Comunicação, Administração e Turismo (administration, tourism, communication, sport, marketing), Mirandela. Email: esact@ipb.pt / academicos-esact@ipb.pt. Website: https://esact.ipb.pt

Finding a specific professor's email or phone: IPB does not publish one central list. Use the staff/people directory ("Docentes" or "Contactos") on the website of the school that teaches the course unit, or ask that school's secretariat by email (addresses above) — they give you the professor's institutional email and office phone.

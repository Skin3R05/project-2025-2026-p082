IPB Tuition Fees (Propinas) 2025/2026

International students (Estatuto de Estudante Internacional):
- Full-time CTeSP, Licenciatura (undergraduate) and Mestrado (master): 1.567,50 € per year (monthly instalment 156,75 €).
- Full-time Doutoramento (doctorate): 2.750,00 € per year (monthly instalment 275,00 €).
- Part-time CTeSP, Licenciatura and Mestrado: 1.045,00 € per year (monthly instalment 104,50 €).
- The total fee may be paid in 10 monthly instalments, each due by the 7th of the month, from October to July.

National students - single curricular units (unidades curriculares avulsas, for students not enrolled in an IPB course):
- CTeSP, Licenciatura, Mestrado: fixed component 348,33 €, plus 11,61 € per ECTS credit (total 418 € / 487 € / 557 € / 627 € / 696 € for 6 / 12 / 18 / 24 / 30 ECTS).
- Undergraduate units for students enrolled in an IPB CTeSP: 11,61 € per ECTS credit.
- Microcredentials: 100,00 € per ECTS credit.

Erasmus and other incoming mobility students are not charged any tuition fee.

For the full rules, see the Regulamento de Propinas and the Regulamento Geral de Matrículas e Inscrições do IPB.

IPB Academic Calendar 2025/2026 (CTeSP, Licenciaturas and Mestrados)

First semester:
- Integration week: 8 September 2025 to 13 September 2025
- Classes start: 15 September 2025
- Christmas break: 22 December 2025 to 3 January 2026
- Academic break: 12 to 17 January 2026
- Exam period: 19 January to 14 February 2026
- End of first semester: 14 February 2026

Second semester:
- Classes start: 16 February 2026
- Easter break: 30 March 2026 to 6 April 2026
- Academic break: 8 to 13 June 2026
- Exam period: 15 June to 11 July 2026
- End of second semester: 11 July 2026

Special exam periods:
- September special period (for students with special statutes and finalists): exams from 1 to 12 September 2026.
- March special period (for study cycles that end in the first semester): exams from 4 to 14 March 2026.

Each semester lasts 20 weeks including the assessment period and corresponds to 30 ECTS.

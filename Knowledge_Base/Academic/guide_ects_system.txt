This ECTS Guide (www.ipb.pt/ectsguide) provides information on the Polytechnic Institute of Bragança (IPB, Portugal) and contains:
The Information Package / Course Catalogue that introduces the institution, its schools, degrees (including programmes and syllabuses), as well as general information for students;
It also describes the application of the ECTS at IPB, namely the curricular crediting system, the grading and qualification system, the relevant documentation to be used in the mobility period and the diploma supplement.
Additional information on programs and procedures in the field of the Erasmus and other international mobility can be found at the website of the International Relation Office, www.ipb.pt/iro.

The IPB has been awarded with the ECTS Label by the European Commission. The ECTS Label certifies the quality of information on the academic offer and the management of students’ mobility within the European Higher Education Area.

IPB has also been awarded the DS Label. The purpose of the Diploma Supplement (DS) is to provide sufficient independent data to improve the international transparency and fair academic and professional recognition of qualifications.
